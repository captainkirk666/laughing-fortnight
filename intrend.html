IN TREND

The In Trend strategy targets stocks demonstrating consistent upward movement, supported by strong price action and rising volume.

These stocks typically form higher highs and higher lows, reflecting sustained demand and positive momentum.

The goal is to follow established trends and capitalise on continued market strength.
